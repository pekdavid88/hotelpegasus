Explore Corfu Island

Discover the beauty of the Ionian Sea

A Multicultural Charm

Corfu in Greece is strongly influenced by the Venetians, the French and the English, giving the island a cosmopolitan, multicultural charm. From lush green hills to noble mansions and from idyllic beaches to world-class museums, this island has everything it takes to please any type of traveler.

Corfu Town (22 km away): A brilliant base for exploration. Get lost in the narrow cobblestone alleys called “kandounia”, visit the Museum of Asian Art, walk down the famous Liston promenade, or stroll around the massive Spianada square. Moving away from the town, you’ll discover picturesque seaside villages, tiny verdant islets, and wild natural landscapes.

Recommended Nearby Beaches

Paleokastritsa Beach

23 km NW of Corfu Town • Diving Club, Sandy, Family Friendly

Probably the most famous and picturesque beach resort on the island. Huge green cliffs surround the coast, and many mysterious sea caves can be visited by boat trips directly from the shore.

Agios Gordios Beach

16 km SW of Corfu Town • Sandy, Organised, Tourist Facilities

One of the most popular long sandy beaches in Corfu. It is completely surrounded by impressive rock formations, lush greenery, excellent water sports centers, and vibrant beach bars.
